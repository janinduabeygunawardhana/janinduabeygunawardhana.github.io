Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the WakeMe mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: Since WakeMe is a geo-alarm app, we require access to your device's location (including background location) to trigger alarms when you reach your destination. This data is processed locally on your device.

Device Information: We may collect basic technical data like your device model, operating system version, and unique identifiers (such as AdID) to ensure the app works correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the app (the location-based alarm).
  • To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use personal data, including your device's advertising ID, to show personalized ads. You can view their policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store your location history on any external servers. Location data is used in real-time to trigger your alarms. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: wakememobileapp@gmail.com
